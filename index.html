Allie Q's has been Marseilles' Main Street barber for over three decades. The chair has been our calling for sixty years — through every fade, taper, and clean lineup, the standard hasn't moved.

We keep things simple. A good cut, a fair price, and a barbershop that feels like the kind a man remembers his father bringing him to.
